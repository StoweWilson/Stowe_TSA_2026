Housing & Shelter

Overview

The Housing & Shelter page provides centralized information for Boston residents seeking safe and stable housing, whether they are facing an immediate housing crisis or planning for long-term stability. Resources include emergency shelters, transitional housing programs, tenant support, and housing advocacy services. The Boston Commons is committed to helping residents understand their options, protect their rights, and access support with dignity and respect.
