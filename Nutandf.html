Nutrition & Food

The Boston Commons Healthcare page connects residents with accessible, community-based health resources across the city, including clinics, wellness programs, and mental health support. Designed to serve individuals and families regardless of insurance status, this page highlights preventative care, counseling services, and local initiatives that promote overall well-being. Our goal is to make health information easy to find and supportive of Boston’s diverse communities.
